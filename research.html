Research

Our research spans multimodal intelligence, federated learning, Bayesian machine learning, and robust AI systems for dynamic real-world environments.

Multimodal Learning

We study how to model and fuse text, audio, video, image, and other heterogeneous signals for reliable perception and understanding.

Federated Learning

We develop privacy-aware distributed learning methods that can operate under Non-IID data, personalization requirements, and communication constraints.

Bayesian Machine Learning

We investigate Bayesian deep learning and probabilistic inference to improve reliability, calibration, and uncertainty quantification in modern AI systems.

Reliable and Uncertainty-Aware AI

We aim to build AI systems that remain robust under missing modalities, noisy environments, temporal shifts, and real-world deployment challenges.
